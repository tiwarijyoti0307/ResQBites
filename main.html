About ResQBites

ResQBites is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and helping those in need. We provide features such as managing groceries with expiry dates, donating leftover food from restaurants, and suggesting recipes to make the most of your ingredients.

Our Features

Explore the key features of ResQBites:

  • Manage groceries with expiry dates
  • Donate leftover food from restaurants
  • Get recipe suggestions

How It Works

ResQBites makes it easy to:

  1. Add groceries and receive expiry date notifications
  2. Donate or purchase discounted leftover food
  3. Discover new recipes and share your own
